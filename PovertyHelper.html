Basic Poverty Facts

  • In 2016 the poverty rate for people living with disabilities was 21%. That is more than 4 million people.
  • In 2016 1 in every 5 children lived in poverty. That is 21.2%.
  • Less than 10% of the world lives in extreme poverty, surviving on $1.90 a day of less.
  • In 2013 more than 1.3 million people in the Chicago metro area lived in in poverty which was over half the population at the time.
